Space Escape

High Concept

A stealth game where the user tries to escape an alien spaceship. For the most part, the user will have to hide from the aliens that try to hunt them down.


Genre

Top-down, stealth, horror


Platform

As of right now, it's only planned for desktops


Story

The player wakes up, unaware of what has happened and how they're here. They only have one goal in mind: Escape from this unknown spaceship that's swarming with aliens


Aesthetics

I'm aiming for an 8-bit style reminiscent of older games such as space invaders or more modern game that share a similar aesthetic such as stardew valley.


Gameplay

The player must hide from the aliens that survey the level and are able to hide within certain containers when spotted. Enemies will be able to spot the player if they're within their vision range and begin to chase them. As the player progresses, more enemies will start to patrol each level in order to stop the player from escaping.

Controls will be stated on the start screen of the game. The controls will probably be the standard WASD movement with spacebar to hide behind designated pieces of cover.
